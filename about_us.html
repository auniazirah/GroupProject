What is Shakerz?

Shakerz is more than just a drink — it's an experience. A deep-flavored beverage designed to be enjoyed cold, Shakerz is at its best when semi-frozen. No brewing, no hassle — just shake it, open it, and enjoy! Our motto, "Goncang Terus Minum" (Shake It, Drink It), reflects our commitment to simplicity and convenience in every sip.

Our Vision

We aim to be the world's leading provider of unique and refreshing beverage solutions. Whether it's quenching your thirst on a hot day or giving you a moment of joy, Shakerz is here to enhance your moments of happiness and refreshment — on a global scale.

Our Mission

At Shakerz, we are dedicated to creating drinks that bring joy and relaxation. Our mission is centered around:

  • Delivering outstanding customer experiences every step of the way.
  • Promoting sustainability and strengthening local communities.
  • Innovating to bring exciting new flavors and experiences to our customers.
  • Building meaningful and lasting relationships with our customers.

Through innovation, sustainability, and a customer-first approach, we are reshaping how beverages are experienced worldwide.
